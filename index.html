OUR EXPERTISE

Our interdisciplinary research spans a range of domains, including learning analytics and educational data mining, trustworthy and explainable AI in education, and data visualization for uncovering actionable learning insights. We are particularly committed to the design of equitable educational technologies that support diverse learner needs, and explore how human‚ÄìAI collaboration can be optimized to enhance teaching and learning experiences in real-world educational contexts. Biography

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the Instructional Technology Program at College of Education at the University of South Florida, Tampa, USA. I do interdisciplinary research in Computer Science and Education, seeking how to enhance the use of AI technologies in the actual teaching and learning settings. I am passionate about solving educational problems with data-driven approaches and telling educational stories with engaging and effective visualization strategies. I investigate analytical models/approaches to better model students' learning processes using large-scale, heterogenous learning data to promote personalized learning
