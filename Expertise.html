OUR EXPERTISE

INTERACTIVE AND ADAPTIVE LEARNING ANALYTICS PLATFORMS

VISTA Lab focuses on the design and implementation of interactive, adaptive learning analytics platforms that better contextualize the role of AI in teaching and learning environments. We investigate how real-time, data-driven feedback systems can support personalized learning, inform instructional practices, and improve learner outcomes. Our work focuses on developing intelligent platforms that:

Adapt to diverse learner needs and contexts

Integrate seamlessly into formal and informal educational settings

Empower educators with actionable insights grounded in pedagogical relevance

Bridge the gap between artificial intelligence and human-centered learning design

We have expertise in AI-driven learner modeling, real-time analytics, dashboard development, and instructional design integration, as well as a strong technical foundation in tools and frameworks such as Python, Flask, PyTorch, large language models (LLMs), and React. This allows us to build platforms that are both technically robust and pedagogically meaningful. We invite students interested in AI, learning analytics, and educational technology to collaborate with us in building the future of adaptive learning environments.

DATA VISUALIZATION IN EDUCATION

Our lab conducts research on educational data visualization. We aim to explore data visualization strategies or tools that can support to promote deeper understanding of learning processes, instructional strategies, and educational equity. Our research is rooted in the belief that well-designed visualizations can transform how educators, students, and stakeholders engage with data to inform practice and improve outcomes.

Enhance data visualization literacy, focusing on the ability to read, interpret, and communicate using visual data.

Design innovative learning experiences that support skill development in data understanding.

Develop pedagogical strategies to boost confidence and competence in interpreting data visuals.

Help educators and students understand the connections between data types, analytical goals, and appropriate visualization techniques.

Our lab has technical expertise in a wide range of visualization tools and platforms, including D3.js, Plotly, Matplotlib, and user-friendly environments like Tableau and PowerBI, enabling us to build scalable, interactive, and context-sensitive visual solutions for diverse educational stakeholders. We welcome students who are passionate about data, design, and education to join us in exploring how visualization can transform teaching and learning.

MEASURING SCIENTIFIC PROGRESS

VISTA Lab works on developing and evaluating approaches to measure scientific progress within a specific research domain. We explore how data-driven methods‚Äîsuch as bibliometric analysis, citation network modeling, and topic evolution tracking‚Äîcan be used to map the structure, growth, and trajectory of scientific knowledge over time. Our research in this area aims to:

Identify emerging trends, research gaps, and influential contributions

Understand the dynamics of interdisciplinary knowledge development

Support strategic decision-making for funding agencies, institutions, and research communities

Provide visual and interactive tools for researchers to explore the evolution of their fields

We have expertise in natural language processing (NLP), network analysis, topic modeling, and interactive visualization tools, leveraging platforms such as Python, PyTorch, LLMs, and D3.js to create meaningful representations of scientific landscapes. We welcome students who are curious about the science of science and want to apply advanced analytical methods to understand how knowledge evolves.
